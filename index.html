PROJECTS

  • Product to pack pills according to the Patient’s prescription — Futuring Design Pvt. Ltd.

    A product to dispense and collate pills in a pouch corresponding to the daily dosage instructions in a patient’s medicine prescription. The output of the product was a reel of pouches of timely doses of medicines. The product was an assemblage of multiple modules working in harmony with each other.

    Design Process: Concept Prototype, Test & Evaluate, Plastic Injection Moulding, Pressure die Cast, Production.

  • Wearable training device for Distance Runners — Futuring Design Pvt. Ltd.

    A product to train and improve the form of distance runners and increase their efficiency. Designed a highly complex mechanism similar in size and complexity of a mechanical wrist watch which could take some force to maintain and improve the runner’s form.

    Design Process: Need validation, Develop and select solutions, test prototypes,Multiple Prototyping processes.

  • Preo | Waste Paper Recycler — M. Des Major

    The Waste Paper Recycler makes use of the office shredded to make a cardboard sheet. Focus of this project is to avoid the use of water for recycling (135 m3 water needed per tonne paper) and close the recycling loop at the source of waste. This product includes a shredder, hopper module, adhesive spray module, conveyor module, pressing/calendaring module and an electronic control unit.

    Design Process: Identify need, Research problem, Develop solutions, Select most promising, Construct a Prototype, Test & Evaluate, Insights, Redesign.

  • Zoola | Smart Baby Cradle — M. Des Minor

    Zoola is a smart cradle for babies between 0 to 2 years of age designed for Indian Market. Zoola operates automatically with simple control and also monitors baby sleep and other factors. It has Two Swinging Motions viz. 1. Side-to-side 2. Head-to-toe

    Design Process: Identify need, Research problem, Develop solutions, Select most promising, Construct a Prototype, Test & Evaluate, Insights, Redesign.
